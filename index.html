Kampung Sungai Melayu

Learn about the village's history, culture and experiences

🏡 History
  • Old Malay fishing village in Johor, Malaysia
  • Started by people who depended on fishing and mangroves
  • Developed into a small village with wooden houses
  • Life stayed simple and closely connected to nature for many years
  • Now known for eco-tourism and mangrove tours

Asam Pedas

Rich, tangy Johor-style spicy dish loved by locals.

Belacan

Belacan

Fermented shrimp paste with strong aroma and deep flavour.

Mussel Chips

Mussel Chips

Crispy seafood snack with a unique salty taste.

Batu Seremban

Batu Seremban

Batu Seremban is a traditional game where players toss and catch small stones or beanbags in stages that test hand-eye coordination.

Ceper

Ceper

Ceper is a simple game where players flick bottle caps to hit targets or reach a goal area with accuracy.

Congkak

Congkak

Congkak is a strategy board game where two players move seeds around holes on a board to collect the most in their home store.

Crab Catching
