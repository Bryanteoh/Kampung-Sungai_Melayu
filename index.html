Kampung Sungai Melayu

🏡 History

Village History

  • Established in the early 1900s by Malay settlers
  • Originally a fishing and farming village
  • Grew due to its coastal location and natural resources
  • Later developed into an eco-tourism destination
🎯 Activities

Things To Do

  • Guided boat tours
  • Traditional fishing
  • Mangrove forest walks
  • Village sightseeing
  • Crab catching experience
🎭 Culture

Living Culture

  • Congkak — traditional board game
  • Ceper — bottle cap game
  • Community-based village living
  • Zapin — traditional dance
🍲 Local Food

Must-Try Dishes

  • Asam Pedas — spicy tamarind fish
  • Ayam Ungkep — seasoned fried chicken
  • Mussel Chips — local crispy snack
🚤 Tours

Tour Packages
